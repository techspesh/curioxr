Simple Goal

Get 40+ Florida colleges to create 360° virtual tours, deliver Meta Quest 3S headsets to high school counselors, and collect usage data to measure impact on students.

Project Activities

Activity 1: College 360° Video Creation

Objective: Get 40+ Florida colleges to create standardized 360° virtual tour videos.
  • College outreach and participation commitments
  • 360° filming guidelines and equipment recommendations
  • Content template with consistent format
  • Essential campus tour stops

Activity 2: Meta Quest 3S Deployment

Objective: Deliver Meta Quest 3S headsets to counselors with onboarding and WiFi setup.
  • Target high school identification (rural/Title 1 priority)
  • Counselor coordination and simple onboarding
  • IT department WiFi setup and support
  • Headset pre-configuration with content

Activity 3: Data Collection & Reporting

Objective: Collect usage data from counselors about students who used virtual tours.
  • Simple data collection forms for counselors
  • Student demographics and engagement tracking
  • Meta Quest 3S usage analytics
  • Monthly reports for Department of Education
